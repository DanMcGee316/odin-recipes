Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo topped with parmaigiano and parsley.

Ingredients for 4 large servings:

  • 2 Large Chicken Breast
  • 2 Cups Low Sodium Chicken Broth
  • 1 Pound Fettuccine
  • 2 Cups Heavy Cream
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, Very Finely Minced
  • 2 Large Egg Yolks
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Italian Parsley
  • 2 Cups Grated Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Put the 2 Large Chicken Breast and 2 Cups of Chicken Broth in a pot. Bring the pot to a simmer and then reduce the heat to low. Once the heat is low, keep the chicken simmering for a total of 10 minutes. After the 10 minutes take it off the heat and let it cool. Once the chicken is done cooling slice the chicken to your desired size.
  2. Bring some water to a boil and add salt to the water. Once boiling add in the Fettuccini. Also while the pasta is cooking, take the Chicken Broth and reduce to about 1 Cup of liquid. After the Chicken Broth is reduced we are going to add the Minced Garlic, Black Pepper, and the 2 Cups of Heavy Cream. Bring the mixture to a low simmer and then turn off the heat.
  3. Next we are going to temper the 2 Egg Yolks. Take a small spoonfull of the hot cream mixture and mix it in with the Egg Yolks. Once that is incorporated, add in another spoonfull of that mixture. Do that about 3 to 4 more times and then you can wisk in the tempered Yolks into the sauce mixture. Finally add Salt to taste.
  4. Once the Fettuccine is al dente, drain the water and toss the noodles back into that pot. Add in the Chopped Parsley, 1 Cup of the Parmigiano-Reggiano, and all the cream mixture. Stur and let sit for one minute. After one minute add in the rest of the cheese and all the Chicken.
  5. Plate, Garnish and Enjoy!
